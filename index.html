About Glazier Adelaide

Founded in 2005, Glazier Adelaide began with a simple mission: to provide Adelaide residents with superior window repair services they could trust. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the city's most respected glazing companies, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the metropolitan area.

Our core values of integrity, excellence, and customer satisfaction drive everything we do. We believe in using only the highest quality materials, employing certified professionals, and maintaining transparent communication throughout every project. Our commitment to these principles has earned us a reputation as Adelaide's most reliable glazing service provider.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of glass repairs do you handle?

We handle all types of glass repairs including window replacements, double glazing, safety glass installation, and emergency repairs for both residential and commercial properties.

How quickly can you respond to emergency repairs?

We offer 24/7 emergency services with typical response times of 60 minutes or less within the Adelaide metropolitan area.

Do you provide warranties on your work?

Yes, all our repairs come with a minimum 12-month warranty on workmanship, and up to 10 years on materials depending on the product installed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and offer flexible payment plans. For insurance claims, we can bill your insurance company directly.

Are your glaziers licensed and insured?

All our glaziers are fully licensed, insured, and police-checked. We carry comprehensive public liability insurance for your peace of mind.
