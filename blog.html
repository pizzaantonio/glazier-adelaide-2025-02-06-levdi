Common Window Glass Problems and Solutions: A Comprehensive Guide

Window glass problems can significantly impact your home's comfort and energy efficiency. Common issues include foggy double-pane windows caused by seal failures, stress cracks from temperature variations, and impact damage from accidents or debris. Professional solutions range from complete IGU replacement for foggy windows to emergency repairs for broken glass. Regular maintenance, including seal inspections and proper cleaning, can prevent many issues. However, most repairs should be handled by professional glaziers who have the expertise and tools for proper installation. When you notice cracks, condensation between panes, or drafts, it's time to call an expert.
